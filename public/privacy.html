Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Better Dog Social Dashboard is an internal reporting dashboard used by Better Dog Supplements and its agency team to review social media performance from connected accounts.

Information We Access

When an authorized account connects a social platform, the dashboard may access public profile information, public video or post information, and performance metrics made available by that platform's API.

How We Use Information

Information is used only to prepare internal reporting, measure social media performance, and maintain the dashboard.

Information Sharing

We do not sell social account information. We do not share connected account data with unrelated third parties. Access is limited to people supporting Better Dog Supplements reporting and dashboard operations.

Data Storage

API credentials are stored as encrypted repository secrets in GitHub Actions. Dashboard data is published only for reporting use within the Better Dog Social Dashboard.

Revoking Access

A connected user may revoke platform access through the relevant platform account settings, including TikTok account app permissions.

Contact

Questions about this dashboard or privacy policy can be directed to the Better Dog Supplements agency team.
