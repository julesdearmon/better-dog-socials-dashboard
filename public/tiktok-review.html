Better Dog Social Dashboard

This dashboard lets the Better Dog Supplements team review social media performance in one place. It currently displays reporting for Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube. TikTok access is pending app review, and the section below shows exactly where authorized TikTok account data will appear after approval.

Demo flow for review

1
Open the dashboardThe website loads the reporting dashboard for Better Dog Supplements.
2
Review current reportingInstagram, Facebook, and YouTube metrics appear in the dashboard for the internal team.
3
Connect TikTok after approvalAn authorized Better Dog TikTok user will grant access using TikTok Login Kit.
4
Show TikTok reportingThe dashboard will display public TikTok video metrics for the authorized account only.
